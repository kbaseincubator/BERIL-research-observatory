# ENIGMA Tenant and Contaminated-Site Context

## Role In The Observatory

ENIGMA data provides a field context for lab and genome-derived predictions. It is especially valuable for validating metal tolerance, plume ecology, redox gradients, and community functional potential.

## High-Value Use

The tenant becomes most valuable when chemistry, community, genome, and sample metadata are linked at the same site and date. That is why missing SSO geochemistry is a high-priority gap.
