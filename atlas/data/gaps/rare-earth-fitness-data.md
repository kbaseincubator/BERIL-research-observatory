# Rare Earth Fitness Data Gap

## Gap

Current metal fitness resources do not appear to include rare-earth-element RB-TnSeq experiments.

## Why It Matters

Rare-earth biology is directly relevant to critical-mineral recovery, but without direct assays, any candidate list must be generated by cross-metal inference and then experimentally validated.

## Agent Use

Use this page to avoid overstating REE claims. Proposals should produce ranked candidates for first experiments, not claim validated REE tolerance mechanisms.
