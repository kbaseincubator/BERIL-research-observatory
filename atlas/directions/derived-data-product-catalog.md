# Derived data product catalog

## Direction

Promote reusable outputs such as scores, labels, mappings, modules, and candidate lists into Atlas-managed data product pages.

## Why It Is High Value

Projects repeatedly create intermediate artifacts that future projects need. Without a catalog, agents rediscover or duplicate them.

## Minimum Product

Each derived product page should name the object, source project, raw collections, identifiers, recomputation path, downstream reuse, caveats, and owner/reviewer.
