# Progressive Disclosure Method

## Method

Every Atlas path should reveal only the next useful level of detail:

1. **Atlas**: what exists and why it matters.
2. **Topic**: a narrative synthesis across projects.
3. **Claim**: one reusable thing we think we know.
4. **Direction**: a valuable next research path.
5. **Hypothesis**: a testable unit.
6. **Data product or join**: reusable operational substrate.
7. **Source**: the original project, doc, collection, or caveat.

## Page Rule

Each page must answer:

- Why does this page exist?
- What sources does it integrate?
- What should a human or agent do with it?

## Anti-Pattern

Do not dump project summaries into topic pages. Topic pages should synthesize across projects and send readers deeper only when needed.
