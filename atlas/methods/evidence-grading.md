# Evidence Grading

## Confidence Labels

- **High**: supported by multiple independent evidence streams or strong methodological repair.
- **Medium**: supported by project outputs but still dependent on coverage, metadata, or interpretation caveats.
- **Low**: plausible synthesis, prediction, or direction that needs explicit testing.

## Required Caveat Discipline

Pages with numbers, rankings, or intervention implications must carry source projects and caveats. Claims from high-stakes domains need adversarial or independent review.

## Agent Rule

If evidence is mostly extrapolated, write a hypothesis or direction page, not a claim page.
