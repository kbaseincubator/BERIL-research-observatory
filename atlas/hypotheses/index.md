# Testable Hypotheses

## What This Section Is For

Hypotheses are agent-sized research units. Each one should identify a testable statement, the required data, expected output, caveats, and the parent claim or direction.

## How To Use Hypotheses

Use this section when turning Atlas synthesis into executable work. A hypothesis should be specific enough that a project plan can name the collections, joins, controls, and success criteria.

## Maintenance Rule

Retire, split, or promote hypotheses when projects test them. The Atlas should preserve both confirmed findings and negative results that prevent repeated work.
