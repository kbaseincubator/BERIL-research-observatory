# Paramvir S. Dehal

## Overview

Paramvir S. Dehal is the central contributor across this demo. The associated
projects cover both themes: metal-resistance synthesis and microbial ecotype
analysis. That makes this author page useful as a contributor-centered map of
how the same pangenome and fitness resources support different biological
questions.

## Projects

On the metal side, Dehal contributes to the atlas, cross-resistance, and
specificity projects. These projects form a sequence: identify metal-important
genes across organisms, compare how metal responses correlate, and then separate
metal-specific genes from general stress effects.

On the ecotype side, Dehal contributes the broad correlation analysis that tests
whether environment or phylogeny better predicts bacterial gene-content
similarity. That project sets up the later functional-differentiation question by
arguing that ecological signal may be concentrated in specific gene subsets.
