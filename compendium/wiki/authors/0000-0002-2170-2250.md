# Justin Reese

## Overview

Justin Reese contributes the ecotype functional differentiation project in this
demo. The project takes the broad ecotype question and makes it more concrete:
after gene-content ecotypes are detected, do those ecotypes differ in the
functions they carry?

## Projects

The associated project, Ecotype Functional Differentiation, finds valid
gene-content ecotypes in most sampled species and shows that their COG
functional profiles differ systematically. Adaptive categories have larger
effect sizes than housekeeping categories, while unknown-function and
mobile-element categories remain major parts of the signal.
