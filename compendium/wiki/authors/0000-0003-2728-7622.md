# Adam Deutschbauer

## Overview

Adam Deutschbauer contributes to the Pan-Bacterial Metal Fitness Atlas in this
demo. That project supplies the broad foundation for the metal-resistance topic:
a cross-species set of metal fitness experiments and the core-genome robustness
claim built from them.

## Projects

The atlas identifies metal-important genes, conserved metal gene families, and
novel metal-biology candidates. Its pangenome-scale scoring analysis also shows
why gene-count signatures need genome-size normalization before they can be used
to reason about specialist taxa.

## Topics

This author page links to [Metal Resistance & Critical Minerals](../topics/metal-resistance.md).
The project draws on [KEScience Fitness Browser](../data/kescience-fitnessbrowser.md)
