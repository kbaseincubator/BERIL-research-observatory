# Entity: Fba Model

## Introduction

The FBA model entity captures the wiki's model-quality concerns. It links gapfilling dependence, weak FBA-class prediction of measured growth defects, FBA-TnSeq discordance, and aromatic media refinement [stmt:adp1-explorer-gapfilling-caveat; acinetobacter_adp1_explorer] [stmt:adp1-triple-fba-growth-caveat; adp1_triple_essentiality].

## Synthesis

The model should be treated as a refinement target. Explorer evidence says model-based growth predictions are limited by heavy gapfilling dependence, while triple-essentiality evidence says FBA class does not predict which TnSeq-dispensable genes show measured growth defects [stmt:adp1-explorer-gapfilling-caveat; acinetobacter_adp1_explorer] [stmt:adp1-triple-fba-growth-caveat; adp1_triple_essentiality].

The constructive path is to prioritize FBA-TnSeq discordant genes and focus on aromatic degradation, where enriched discordance suggests a systematic model gap. Trace aromatic compounds in media definitions are the proposed retest [stmt:adp1-explorer-discordance-opportunity; acinetobacter_adp1_explorer] [stmt:adp1-triple-aromatic-discordance-finding; adp1_triple_essentiality] [stmt:adp1-triple-aromatic-media-opportunity; adp1_triple_essentiality].
