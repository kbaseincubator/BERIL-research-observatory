# Topic: Adp1 Carbon Fitness

## Introduction

ADP1 carbon fitness links deletion phenotyping, explorer-derived condition structure, and triple-essentiality evidence into one topic. The topic asks how gene requirements vary across growth conditions and which measurements should drive synthesis [stmt:adp1-deletion-continuum-claim; adp1_deletion_phenotypes] [stmt:adp1-explorer-condition-fitness-finding; acinetobacter_adp1_explorer] [stmt:adp1-triple-continuous-fitness-claim; adp1_triple_essentiality].

## Synthesis

The deletion project frames ADP1 fitness as a continuous phenotype landscape with quinate degradation as the discrete exception. It supports that claim with growth-defect tiers across eight carbon sources, independent condition dimensions, and a quinate module that stands apart from the broader continuous pattern [stmt:adp1-deletion-continuum-claim; adp1_deletion_phenotypes] [stmt:adp1-deletion-carbon-tier-finding; adp1_deletion_phenotypes] [stmt:adp1-deletion-condition-independence-finding; adp1_deletion_phenotypes] [stmt:adp1-deletion-quinate-module-finding; adp1_deletion_phenotypes].

The explorer broadens this topic by showing that mutant growth fitness has condition-specific structure, with urea and quinate behaving as outlier conditions. That finding makes the carbon-fitness topic more than a deletion-only summary: it points to condition-specific modules that can be followed through data-integration pages [stmt:adp1-explorer-condition-fitness-finding; acinetobacter_adp1_explorer].

The triple-essentiality project supplies measurement guidance. Continuous fitness values outperform binary essentiality fractions, so this topic should preserve quantitative phenotypes when comparing conditions, models, and proteomics [stmt:adp1-triple-continuous-fitness-claim; adp1_triple_essentiality] [stmt:adp1-triple-fitness-predictor-finding; adp1_triple_essentiality].

The next work is to expand the carbon-source panel, analyze urea as an independent module, and test media refinements around aromatic degradation. These opportunities keep the topic tied to experiments and model updates rather than only retrospective synthesis [stmt:adp1-deletion-expand-carbon-panel-opportunity; adp1_deletion_phenotypes] [stmt:adp1-explorer-urea-deep-dive-opportunity; acinetobacter_adp1_explorer] [stmt:adp1-triple-aromatic-media-opportunity; adp1_triple_essentiality].
