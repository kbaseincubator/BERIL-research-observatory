# ADP1 FBA media definitions should be refined with trace aromatic compounds and retested against aromatic-degradation discordance.

## Introduction

This opportunity proposes a concrete model-quality experiment: add trace aromatic compounds to ADP1 FBA media definitions and retest the model against aromatic-degradation discordance [stmt:adp1-triple-aromatic-media-opportunity; adp1_triple_essentiality].

## Synthesis

The proposal is motivated by the finding that aromatic degradation genes are enriched among ADP1 FBA-discordant genes. If the discrepancy is partly media-driven, trace aromatic compounds provide a targeted way to test that model gap [stmt:adp1-triple-aromatic-discordance-finding; adp1_triple_essentiality] [stmt:adp1-triple-aromatic-media-opportunity; adp1_triple_essentiality].

The caveat is that FBA class alone does not predict which TnSeq-dispensable genes have measured growth defects. Retesting should therefore compare model changes against continuous fitness and orthogonal evidence rather than declaring success from model agreement alone [stmt:adp1-triple-fba-growth-caveat; adp1_triple_essentiality] [stmt:adp1-triple-continuous-fitness-claim; adp1_triple_essentiality].

The opportunity connects back to deletion and explorer synthesis: continuous carbon-fitness structure and the explorer's BERDL bridge both make the experiment easier to interpret across projects [stmt:adp1-deletion-continuum-claim; adp1_deletion_phenotypes] [stmt:adp1-explorer-database-bridge-claim; acinetobacter_adp1_explorer].
