# FBA-TnSeq discordant genes in ADP1 should be prioritized for metabolic model refinement.

## Introduction

This opportunity turns ADP1 model disagreement into a triage list. It proposes prioritizing FBA-TnSeq discordant genes for metabolic model refinement [stmt:adp1-explorer-discordance-opportunity; acinetobacter_adp1_explorer].

## Synthesis

The opportunity is useful because the explorer is already positioned as a BERDL bridge, but model predictions are limited by heavy dependence on gapfilled reactions. Discordant genes are therefore candidates for targeted refinement, not evidence to accept uncritically [stmt:adp1-explorer-database-bridge-claim; acinetobacter_adp1_explorer] [stmt:adp1-explorer-gapfilling-caveat; acinetobacter_adp1_explorer].

The cross-project context strengthens this caution. ADP1 synthesis should prioritize continuous fitness and orthogonal evidence, and the deletion project treats condition-dependent essentiality as a continuous landscape rather than a simple categorical model outcome [stmt:adp1-triple-continuous-fitness-claim; adp1_triple_essentiality] [stmt:adp1-deletion-continuum-claim; adp1_deletion_phenotypes].
