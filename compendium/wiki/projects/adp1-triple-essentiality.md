# Project: Adp1 Triple Essentiality

## Introduction

The ADP1 triple-essentiality project contributes quantitative measurement guidance and model-quality caveats. It argues that ADP1 synthesis should prioritize continuous fitness and orthogonal evidence over binary essentiality thresholds alone [stmt:adp1-triple-continuous-fitness-claim; adp1_triple_essentiality].

## Synthesis

The project's positive evidence is that continuous fitness values outperform binary essentiality fractions for predicting ADP1 essentiality, while proteomics is a strong signal that performs comparably to continuous fitness in ROC analysis [stmt:adp1-triple-fitness-predictor-finding; adp1_triple_essentiality] [stmt:adp1-triple-proteomics-finding; adp1_triple_essentiality].

The project also defines model-quality limits. FBA class does not predict which TnSeq-dispensable genes have measured growth defects, and aromatic degradation genes are enriched among FBA-discordant genes [stmt:adp1-triple-fba-growth-caveat; adp1_triple_essentiality] [stmt:adp1-triple-aromatic-discordance-finding; adp1_triple_essentiality].

Its follow-up is to refine ADP1 FBA media definitions with trace aromatic compounds and retest against aromatic-degradation discordance [stmt:adp1-triple-aromatic-media-opportunity; adp1_triple_essentiality].
