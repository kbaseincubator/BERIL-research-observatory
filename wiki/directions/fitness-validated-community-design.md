# Fitness-validated community design

## Direction

Select organisms or strains for a target environment using measured fitness, metal tolerance, metabolic capabilities, dependencies, and AMR/ecological risk.

## Why It Is High Value

It converts observatory outputs into design rules. This could support remediation, formulation, or synthetic ecology.

## Minimum Product

A design table where each candidate taxon has tolerance scores, metabolic capabilities, dependency evidence, environmental fit, and risk annotations.
