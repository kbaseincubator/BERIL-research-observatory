# Missing SSO Geochemistry

## Gap

The discoveries log records that SSO sample tubes for metals, ICTOC, isotopes, and nitrogen species are registered, but linked geochemistry assay values were not ingested.

## Why It Matters

This is the missing validation layer for plume, metal, and redox ecology. Closing the gap would let agents test community predictions against measured chemistry rather than inferred gradients.

## Agent Use
