# Open pangenomes carry broader pathway diversity

## Hypothesis

Open pangenomes have more diverse metabolic pathway repertoires because accessory gene flow expands niche breadth.

## Testable With

Pangenome openness metrics, GapMind pathway summaries, genome-count controls, and taxonomic stratification.

## Caveat

Sampling depth can create apparent openness and apparent pathway diversity simultaneously.
