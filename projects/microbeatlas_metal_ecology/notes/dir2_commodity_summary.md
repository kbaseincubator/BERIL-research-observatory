# Direction 2: Mine Commodity Type × Richness Suppression — 2026-04-04

## Hypothesis
If metal toxicity drives the H1 richness suppression, then mines extracting the most
bactericidal metals (Cu, Hg, Pb-Zn) should produce greater near-vs-mid richness
fold-changes than mines of inert commodities (sand/gravel, limestone).
