# H3: Arctic CWM Metal-Type Diversity — 2026-04-04

## Approach
Community-weighted mean (CWM) metal-type diversity was computed for 288 samples
(n_Arctic = 143, n_Temperate = 145).
Samples were drawn randomly from `enriched_metadata` (lat > 60 Arctic; 20–60 temperate;
seq depth ≥ 1,000 reads). OTU relative abundances were merged with metal AMR annotations
from `otu_pangenome_link.csv` (12,438 OTUs with GTDB pangenome links).

CWM = Σ(rel_abund_i × n_metal_types_i) / Σ(rel_abund_i for annotated OTUs)

## Interpretation
No significant difference in CWM metal-type diversity between Arctic and temperate communities (MW p = 0.3402). This suggests that either (a) the geogenic metal signal at Arctic latitudes does not strongly select for metal resistance at the community level, or (b) sample composition (dominated by aquatic environments) dilutes any soil-driven effect. Environment-stratified analysis (Fig B) provides additional context.
