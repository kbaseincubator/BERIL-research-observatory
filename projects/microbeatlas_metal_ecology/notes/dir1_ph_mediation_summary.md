# Direction 1: pH Mediation of Mine-Proximity Richness Depression — 2026-04-04

## Hypothesis
Soil acidification from mine drainage (acid mine drainage, AMD) may be the true proximate
cause of reduced OTU richness near mines, rather than direct metal toxicity. If pH is a
mediator, then controlling for pH should eliminate or substantially attenuate the mine-distance
coefficient.
