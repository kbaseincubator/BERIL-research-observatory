# References

## Primary Data Sources

- **ENIGMA Genome Depot** — 3,110 cultured isolate genomes from the ENIGMA project, accessible via BERDL `enigma_genome_depot_enigma` tables.
- **ENIGMA CORAL** — Environmental data and MAG bins from the Oak Ridge Field Research Center, accessible via BERDL `enigma_coral` tables.
- **KBase Pangenome** — GTDB-derived species pangenomes with bakta annotations, accessible via BERDL `kbase_ke_pangenome` tables.
