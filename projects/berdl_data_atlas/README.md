# BERDL Data Atlas — Inventory, Topic Map, and Cross-Reference Synergies

## Research Question
What data is available in BERDL (across tenants, agencies, and programs), what
biological topics does it cover, and where do datasets amplify each other when
combined? Build a depiction that serves both KBase users picking what to
analyze and funders / PIs evaluating cross-agency, cross-program synergies.

## Status
Exploration — research plan not yet written.

## Overview
KBase BERDL hosts 1,531 tables across 14 tenants (NMDC, ENIGMA, PROTECT, NETL,
USGS, Planet Microbe, Phage Foundry, KBase, KBase KE Science, ESE, Arkin Lab,
Microbial Ecosystems Lab, refdata, "u") spanning agencies and programs from
DOE, NIH, NSF, ARPA-H, USGS, and partner labs. The catalog has been published
per-database (`data/berdl_inventory.md`) but a cohesive depiction of (a) topic
coverage and (b) cross-reference opportunities does not yet exist. This project
builds that depiction in three layers: catalog + topic map, linkage atlas
grounded in shared join keys, and an audit of realized cross-references from
the ~70 existing BERIL projects.
